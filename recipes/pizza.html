Humus pizza

Description.

This pizza uses hummus instead of the usual red sauceâ€”a unique and healthy pizza for those bored with the traditional. Top with your favorite veggies and cheese.

    Ingredients
  • 1 (10 ounce) can of refrigerated pizza crust dough.
  • 1 cup of hummus spread.
  • 1 cups of sliced bell peppers, any color.
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets.
  • 200 gr Mozzarella cheese.
  • 100 gr of your favorite mushrooms.
    Steps to follow.
  1. Preheat the oven at 475 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Roll out pizza crust and place it on a pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread a thin layer of hummus over the crust. Arrange sliced peppers,broccoli and mushroom over the hummus, and top with cheese mozzarella.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes. Slice and serve.
