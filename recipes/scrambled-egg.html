Sardine eggs scramble.

Description.

Sardine eggs scramble! it's such tasty food and full of protein.it's a typical dish from Venezuela.

    Ingredients.
  • 4 eggs.
  • 1 Sardine can with tomato sauce.
  • 1 onion.
  • salt.
    Steps to follow.
  1. Chop the onion to fry.
  2. Fry the onion and when it's gold drop the sardine can with the sauce.
  3. Scrambled the 4 eggs in a bowl and drop it together with the onion and the sardines.
  4. continue scrambling until it's well cooked.
