Arepa.

Description.

Delicious for breakfast or dinner.It's easy to do and has different stuffing.

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup of white cornmeal (Harina P.A.N is the best choice.).
  • 1 cup of water.
  • Salt to taste.
    Steps to follow.
  1. On a bowl throw the water and salt to taste.
  2. put the cornmeal in the bowl. Then knead it around at 5 minutes until the dough be enough soft to manipulate it.
  3. Do a balls of dough and flatten it until you create a dough with circle shape.
  4. put the circles of dough on a cooking plate in mid fire.
  5. cook it around 3-5 minute for each side.
